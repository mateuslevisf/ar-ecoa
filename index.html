How to Use This AR Experience

  1. Print or Display Patterns: You can either print the patterns on paper or display them on another screen
  2. Allow Camera Access: When prompted, grant camera permission to your browser
  3. Point Your Camera: Aim your device's camera at any of the patterns above
  4. Watch the Magic: 3D objects will appear overlaid on the real-world patterns
  5. Move Around: Try moving your device to see the 3D objects from different angles

Requirements

  • Modern web browser with camera support
  • HTTPS connection (required for camera access)
  • Good lighting for optimal pattern detection
  • Printed patterns or secondary display device
